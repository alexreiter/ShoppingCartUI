Got Question?

Does TELCO offer any other rate plans as a result of the merger with Sprint?

Your whole family can talk, text, and surf on their smartphones without worrying about what they’ll pay. To support our price commitment, we may make other select rate plans available for customers who prefer the features of these older plans—the plans are available for a limited time as we continue to launch plans that are the same or better.

Are there any taxes and fees not included with my Essential Plan?

All applicable monthly regulatory fees and sales taxes on your plan are included with Magenta. This feature applies to your monthly service price. You may see separate taxes and/or fees on other things, for example: Activities that aren’t part of your monthly service, such as buying extra data passes, late or restore fees, device upgrade support charges, calling from foreign countries, etc. Device and accessory purchases, plus SIM starter kits, will continue to be taxed at time of purchase.

What is Spotify on Us?

Through an exclusive partnership with Spotify, you can get our Essential plan with Spotify Basic included at no additional cost (up to 89 kr/mo.).

Will I automatically have Spotify included on my plan? Do I have to change my TELCO plan to get it?

If you have two or more Essential or Plus voice lines, a Spotify on us feature will automatically be available on your account, In order to get the Spotify on Us feature, you'll need to opt into it.

How do I opt in to get Spotify at no additional charge? How does it work?

If you’re an existing customer, visit MyTelco.com, a retail store, or call Customer Care to activate. If you’re a new customer, simply visit a TELCO store, go to TELCO.com. You’ll be given a URL to link your TELCO account with your Spotify account. TELCO covers the cost of Spotify Basic, up to 89kr, for you. For existing Spotify customers, it may take 1-2 Spotify billing cycles for your Spotify billing to transfer to TELCO.
